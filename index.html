Mid South Developers of La. is a trusted name for commercial and residential construction needs. Based in Metairie, La., we offer new construction and home remodeling services for New Orleans and the Louisiana area. Our 15 years of highly reputable successful roofing, home remodeling and new construction in Louisiana has earned us an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau. We are dedicated to meeting all of your building needs in an efficient and professional manner.

New construction projects in New Orleans and throughout Louisiana require the highest levels of skill and customer service to ensure the job is done right the first time. We are experienced in home remodeling, which includes every room in the house such as bathrooms and kitchens. Additions to existing structures and roofing are also some of the specialties we are trusted with by many satisfied and repeat customers. For everything from new house construction to commercial property renovation, Mid South Developers of La., Inc, offers reliable and cost effective options. As a total construction service company we can tailor a variety of roofing, structural and renovation services to complement existing structures.

Our years of experience working in the New Orleans area gives us a strong understanding of the relevant building codes and specifications necessary to make your new home construction, home renovation or commercial property expansion fully compliant. Whether you are endeavoring on a new project or rejuvenating and existing property, our experienced and skilled staff can help. We are available Monday through Friday from 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. to work with you by explaining building options and offering the benefit of our 15 years of experience working in the Louisiana area.
